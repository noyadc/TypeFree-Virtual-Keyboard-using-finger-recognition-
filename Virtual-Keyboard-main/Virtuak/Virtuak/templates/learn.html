Best Practices for Optimal Performance

💡

Lighting Setup

Ensure even, bright lighting without harsh shadows. Natural light works best, but avoid direct sunlight on your hands.

  • Use diffused lighting
  • Avoid backlighting
  • Maintain consistent brightness
📷

Camera Positioning

Position your camera at eye level or slightly above. Keep your hands clearly visible in the frame.

  • 2-3 feet distance from camera
  • Hands centered in frame
  • Stable camera position
✋

Hand Gestures

Use clear, deliberate gestures. Keep your movements smooth and avoid rapid, jerky motions.

  • Point with index finger
  • Make fist to click
  • Keep gestures consistent
