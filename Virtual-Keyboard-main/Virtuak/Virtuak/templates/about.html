Our Mission

At TypeFree, we believe technology should adapt to humans, not the other way around. Our mission is to create intuitive, accessible, and hygienic ways to interact with digital devices through advanced hand gesture recognition and AI-powered interfaces.

👁️

Our Vision

We envision a world where physical barriers disappear, where anyone can interact with technology naturally and efficiently. From virtual keyboards to gesture-controlled whiteboards, we're building the future of human-computer interaction.
